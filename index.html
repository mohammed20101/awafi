Our Story

Founded in 2015, Awafi Coffee began as a small family-owned café in the heart of Riyadh. Our name "Awafi" translates to "loyal" in Arabic, reflecting our commitment to authentic flavors and warm hospitality.

We source our coffee beans directly from Yemeni and Ethiopian farmers, ensuring the highest quality while supporting sustainable practices. Our pastries and dishes incorporate traditional Middle Eastern ingredients with a modern twist.

More than just a coffee shop, Awafi is a gathering place where friends meet, ideas flow, and the rich aroma of freshly brewed coffee fills the air with comfort and inspiration.
